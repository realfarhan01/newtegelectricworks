ABOUT NEWTEG ELECTRIC WORKS INDUSTRY

M/s New Teg Electric Works having its registered office and works at D-8 A RIICO Industrial area phase -1 was established by Mr. Shanti. Lal baid in the year 2002,having experience in the transformer industry for more than 30years.
